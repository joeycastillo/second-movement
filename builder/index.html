        HOMEBASE LOCATION
        WHAT IS HOMEBASE?

        Homebase is your primary location for circadian-aware features.

        The watch uses your coordinates to generate a seasonal table with:

        • Expected daylight hours for each day
        • Typical temperatures by season
        • Circadian energy baselines

        This enables phase engine watch faces to track your natural rhythms and provide context-aware recommendations.

        Only needed for phase-aware faces. Leave blank if not using circadian features (~2KB flash saved).

        LATITUDE
        Your location's north-south position (-90 to +90)
        Examples: Anchorage: 61.2181 • Seattle: 47.6062 • NYC: 40.7128
        LONGITUDE
        Your location's east-west position (-180 to +180)
        Examples: Anchorage: -149.9003 • Seattle: -122.3321 • NYC: -74.0060
        TIMEZONE
        Standard timezone code or UTC offset
        Formats: AKST, PST, EST, HST, UTC-9, UTC+3, or -540 (minutes from UTC)
        ⚠ Use standard time (not daylight saving)
        ZONE CONFIGURATION

        Customize phase zone boundaries (optional - defaults work for most users)

        WHAT ARE ZONES?

        The phase engine divides your day into 4 zones based on your phase score (0-100):

        • Emergence (0-25): Waking, orienting - focus on rest quality
        • Momentum (26-50): Building energy - wake momentum key
        • Active (51-75): Peak output - energy dominates
        • Descent (76-100): Winding down - comfort priority
        EMERGENCE/MOMENTUM
